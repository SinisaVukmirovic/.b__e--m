I am a light card

Card is a BLOCK.

Card image, card title and card body (content) are ELEMENTS.

Card--light and card--dark are MODIFIERS.

I am a dark card

Card is a BLOCK.

Card image, card title and card body (content) are ELEMENTS.

Card--light and card--dark are MODIFIERS.

Block in this example would be the entire card.

Double underscore (__) denotes an element that lives inside a block. In this example card image, card title and card body (content) are all element inside of a card block.

Double hiphen (--) are modification to that block. In this example, light and dark type of cards.
